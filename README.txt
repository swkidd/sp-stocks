S&P 500 Company Stock Info

Sort columns by clicking the 'SORT-BY' buttons or by clicking on column headers.

Search for a company with the search bar.

Choose a company to display:
    earnings data for the past 10 earnings releases
    the average stock price change on the day the earning was released
    the average or all changes for the past 10 earnings by point and percentage
    an interactive stock chart marking the past earnings

Use the toolbar under the stock on the earnings detail page to chart to zoom, move and save the chart
Each blue marker on the chart marks the daily candlestick for that earnings release

Click the 'HOME' button to return to the front page at any time and the 'EXIT' buttons to exit the application

Click the 'HELP' button to display this page

DATA SOURCES:
    Historical stock data is pulled from Yahoo Finance using the 'yfinance' python library (https://pypi.org/project/yfinance/)
    Earnings dates and real time stock quotes are pulled from Zacks.com
        earnings anouncements (ex: https://www.zacks.com/stock/research/AAPL/earnings-announcements)
        next earnings dates (ex: https://www.zacks.com/stock/quote/AAPL/detailed-estimates)
        stock quotes (ex: http://quote-feed.zacks.com/?t=AAPL)
    Current S&P 500 Company information is pulled from wikipedia (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_S%26P_500_companies)
    Company summaries are pulled from MarketWatch (ex: https://www.marketwatch.com/investing/stock/AAPL)
